Apple Bavarian Torte (★★★★☆)

A classic European torte baked in a springform pan. Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apples make this the perfect holiday treat (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 cups thinly sliced apples
  • 1/3 cup sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the butter and 1/2 cup sugar. Stir in the vanilla. Pat the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat together the cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 teaspoon vanilla, and the egg. Pour this mixture over the crust.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the 1/4 cup sugar and the cinnamon. Toss with apples until coated. Arrange apples in a circular pattern over the cream cheese layer.
  5. Sprinkle with sliced almonds.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C), and continue baking for 25 minutes. Cool before removing from pan.
